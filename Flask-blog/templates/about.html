About

What is Health Hacks?

The Health Hacks blog was launched for the purpose of providing useful insight and data driven information surrounding health and fitness topics. On this blog you can expect to find posts regarding athletic training protocols, nutrition, neuroscience, and disease mitigation and prevention. Using academic research, data analytics, and experience, Health Hacks hopes to bring you valuable insights which allow you to lead a healthier, more active, and fulfilled life.

Who is the Health Hacker?

My name is Leo and I am the creator and curator of Health Hacks. I have always wanted to share valuable, no-nonsense, information surrounding health and fitness. My own experience with sport performance and autoimmune disease has motivated me to do so to help people who are searching for answers as I was. I have intensely researched the fields of Sport and Biological Sciences since the age of 15. I additionally have First Class Honours in Economics and am also a self-taught Python Developer. This site was in fact a full stack development project created by me. Using the experience gained through national level sport performance and training, my interest in biology and neuroscience, and my data science proficiencies, I hope to bring both empirical and anecdotal value to topics surrounding health and athletic performance.
